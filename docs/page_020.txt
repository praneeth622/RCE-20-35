36.   Promotion.   
 
 (1)    An officer of the Service except one of the medical branch shall be eligible for 
substantive promotion to higher rank in time scale to the rank of Lieutenant and Lieutenant 
Commander on completion of three and eleven years of service respectively from the date of 
first commission if such officer - 
 
 (a)    possess es a satisfactory record of service:  
 
 (b)    is regular in attending training periods as laid down from time to time:  
 
 ©    is recommended by the Commanding Officer:  
 
 (d)    has qualified in case of promotion from Sub -Lieutenant to Lieutenant.  
 
 (2)    Substantive Lieutenant Commanders with six to twelve years seniority in that rank 
shall be eligible for promotion to the rank of Commander on the basis of selection.  
 
37. Counting of previous Service.  Previous full time Commission in the Regular Forces or 
previous commissioned service in the Army Reserve, Territorial Army and Indian Air Force 
Auxiliary Service shall be counted for the purpose of promotion.
