16.    Cadre of Officers .  The cadre of officers in the Service shall be as authorized by the 
Government.  
 
17.    Branches.   The Service shall comprise Executive, Engineering, Electrical, Medical and 
Education Branches.  
 
18.    Ranks.   The following ranks are authorized to be held by;  
 
(a) Executive Branch. – Commander, Lieutenant Commander, Lieutenant, Sub -
Lieutenant, Acting Sub -Lieutenant.  
 
(b) Engineering Branch - Commander, Lieutenant Commander, Lieutenant,  Sub-
Lieutenant, Acting Sub -Lieutenant  
 
(c) Electrical Branch -- Commander, Lieutenant Commander, Lieutenant, Sub -
Lieutenant, Acting Sub -Lieutenant  
 
(d) Medical Branch. - Surgeon Commander, Surgeon  Lieutenant, 
Lieutenant,urgeon Lieutenant.  
 
(e) Education Branch - Comman der, Lieutenant Commander, Lieutenant,                    
Sub-Lieutenant, Acting Sub -Lieutenant  
 
19.   Precedence  
1. The order of precedence shall be Indian Navy and Indian Naval Auxiliary 
Service.  
 
2. All officers shall take precedence in the order specified in  sub-regulation (1), 
rank for rank irrespective of seniority.  
 
3. Officers holding equivalent acting higher ranks shall rank among themselves 
according to the date of their substantive seniority.
